The hyperlink a tag or anchor tag allow you navigate to around your page or to a different page.

<a href="www.example.com" target="_blank">something</a>

href is the location of the file or URL you want to goto.
www.example.com is the location of the file.
target is optional and let the browser know how you would like it open the link.
_blank opens the link in a new window or tab.
something is the text the user will click to activate the hyperlink.
